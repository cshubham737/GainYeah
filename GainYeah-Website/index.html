Consistency is a key

Consistency in working out will bring more substantial results than periodic extreme fitness routines. Working out three hours a day for one month can bring on health problems including stress fractures, tendon and muscle strains that often discourage people from continuing to workout. Try slowly increasing work out time weekly or biweekly starting with 30 minutes, three times a week and slowly making it to 60 minutes five times a week.
